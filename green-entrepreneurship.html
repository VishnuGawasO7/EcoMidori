Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you get started with circular entrepreneurship

What makes a business truly circular vs just "green"?

Truly circular ventures design out waste: materials stay in use, products are made for repair & reuse, and business models prioritize access and value retention over single-use sales.

How can startups access funding for circular projects?

Tap climate tech funds, impact investors, corporate partnerships, and targeted grants. EcoMidori connects founders with 50+ active impact investors and provides application templates.

Is circular entrepreneurship actually profitable?

Yes. Circular models reduce raw material costs, open new revenue streams (services, refurbishment), and deepen customer loyaltyâ€”which often leads to faster profitability.

What's the first step to start a circular business?

Run a material flow analysis: map inputs, outputs, and value losses. From there, test small circular interventions (repair programs, take-back pilots) and measure results.
