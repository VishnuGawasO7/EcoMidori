Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about working with us

How quickly will I hear back after submitting my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please indicate this in your message subject line.

Do you offer free initial consultations?

Yes, we offer a complimentary 30-minute consultation to discuss your needs and explore how we can help you implement circular economy principles.

What types of businesses do you work with?

We work with businesses of all sizes and across various industries, from startups to established corporations, who are committed to implementing sustainable and circular practices.

Do you offer virtual consultations for international clients?

Absolutely! We regularly work with clients globally through video conferencing and have flexible scheduling to accommodate different time zones.
