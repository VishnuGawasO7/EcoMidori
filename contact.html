Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our programs and services

How can I apply for the Green Entrepreneurship program?

Applications open quarterly. Visit our Green Entrepreneurship page for details on upcoming cohorts and application deadlines.

Do you offer funding for sustainable startups?

While we don't provide direct funding, we connect qualified ventures with our network of impact investors and grant opportunities.

Can I volunteer with EcoMidori?

Yes! We welcome volunteers with expertise in sustainability, business, design, and education. Contact us to discuss opportunities.

Do you offer custom workshops for organizations?

Absolutely. We develop tailored workshops on circular economy, sustainable design, and green entrepreneurship for businesses and educational institutions.
