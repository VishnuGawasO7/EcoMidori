Our Mission & Vision

Driving sustainable change through innovation and education

Our Mission

To empower communities through green entrepreneurship and circular economy principles, creating sustainable solutions that address environmental challenges while generating economic opportunities.

Our Vision

A world where sustainable business practices are the norm, where communities thrive in harmony with nature, and where every individual has the opportunity to contribute to a circular, regenerative economy.

Our Approach

Combining education, innovation, and community engagement to create practical solutions for sustainable development. We believe in learning by doing and creating impact through collaboration.

Project Journey

From idea to implementation - Our development timeline

January 2024

Ideation Phase

Initial concept development for EcoMidori, focusing on green entrepreneurship and circular economy principles. Research on local environmental challenges in Goa.

  • Market research and problem identification
  • Stakeholder analysis and community needs assessment
  • Initial concept validation with school community
February 2024

Planning & Design

Structured planning of the EcoMidori platform, website design, and content strategy. Development of educational materials and project frameworks.

  • Website architecture and user experience design
  • Content creation for core topics
  • Educational resource development
  • Partnership outreach strategy
March 2024

Development Phase

Technical implementation of the EcoMidori website with focus on responsive design, accessibility, and interactive features.

  • Frontend development with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript
  • Interactive quiz and assessment tools
  • Responsive design implementation
  • Content management system setup
April 2024

Testing & Refinement

Comprehensive testing of all features, content review, and user feedback collection. Iterative improvements based on testing results.

  • User acceptance testing with student groups
  • Content accuracy verification
  • Performance optimization
  • Accessibility compliance testing
May 2024

Launch & Deployment

Official launch of EcoMidori platform with initial project implementations in partner schools and communities.

  • Website deployment and hosting setup
  • School workshops and training sessions
  • Community awareness campaigns
  • Initial project implementations
June 2024 - Ongoing

Growth & Expansion

Continuous improvement, community engagement, and scaling successful initiatives to new regions and schools.

  • Performance monitoring and analytics
  • Community feedback integration
  • Content updates and feature additions
  • Partnership expansion

Our Home Base

Shri Bhumika Higher Secondary School, Parye

About Our School

Shri Bhumika Higher Secondary School in Parye, Sattari, Goa, serves as the incubator for EcoMidori. The school provides a supportive environment for innovation and sustainable initiatives.

Location: Parye, Sattari, Goa, India
Focus Areas: Environmental Education, Sustainable Practices, Community Engagement
Initiatives: Waste Management, Organic Farming, Environmental Clubs
